Covid comparison graphs

Simple graphs that were created during COVID-19 pandemic. Graphs compare Czechia, Italy, Sweden and USA. They should track the trend from the first death and infected, to compare how the pandemic was handled in the different states. And take into account that the pandemic started in each country in different time.

This project is now archived and no new data are added. Big thanks for sharing the data to: Center for Systems Science and Engineering (CSSE) at Johns Hopkins University
